What is most people largest purchase in life?

When buying a new house or condo, clients often only receive a brochure with 2D floorplans. In contrast, for cars, you can test drive, read reviews, etc.

- -

TeSLATe Design



In response, we are 3D-printing scale models of newly constructed homes. Over the past months, we have optmised the process of converting 2D floor plans into 3D virtual models and then into codes for 3D printers, in order to create rapidly and inexpensively scale models in volume.

We are aiming to raise the transparency of information for home-buyers. This will increase buyers' confidence in their own more informed decision as well as in the real-estate developers' modern and sophisticated technology. Furthermore, emotional attachment to the home can be more easily established, given the end-users' ability to touch and customise using 3d printed furniture/fixtures.
